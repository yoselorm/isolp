Join the International Society of Laser Proctology

Become Part of the Global Community

Join our prestigious organization of medical professionals dedicated to advancing the field of laser proctology. As a member, you'll gain access to exclusive resources, networking opportunities, and the latest research developments.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Have experience in managing proctological conditions
  • Possess formal training as a surgeon, gastroenterologist, or dermatologist
  • Have attended at least one laser workshop or training program
  • Demonstrate an active interest in research

Application Process

  1. Confirm Eligibility – Ensure you meet the membership criteria outlined above.
  2. Download the Application Form – Available on our official website.
  3. Complete the Form & Gather Documents – Fill out the application and compile necessary supporting materials.
  4. Submit Your Application – Send the completed form and supporting documents to office@isolp.org.
  5. Application Review – Our team will assess your submission and notify you of the outcome.
